ABOUT

Youth is a time of life where the transition from childhood to maturity takes place. So ARSCPM YOUTH calls on every youth to come together to experience a youthful life style in Christ Jesus in order to abolish boring, frustrated & broken adulthood as parents of next generation.

Mission

The department mission: To engage our community youth in a relationship with God.

Vission

The department Vision: To establish creative & Innovative methods that inspire,excite & Ignite people for Christ Jesus.
